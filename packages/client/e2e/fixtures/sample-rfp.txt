REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL
Marketing Services for Higher Education Institution

SUBMISSION DEADLINE: January 31, 2024

SECTION 1: INTRODUCTION

ABC University is seeking proposals from qualified marketing agencies to provide comprehensive marketing services. This RFP outlines our requirements and evaluation criteria.

SECTION 2: SCOPE OF WORK

The selected vendor will be responsible for:

1. Brand Strategy Development
   Please describe your approach to developing a comprehensive brand strategy for a higher education institution.

2. Digital Marketing Services
   What is your experience with digital marketing campaigns for universities?

3. Content Creation
   How do you ensure content quality and consistency across multiple platforms?

4. Social Media Management
   Describe your social media management process and reporting capabilities.

5. Analytics and Reporting
   What metrics do you track and how do you measure campaign success?

SECTION 3: REQUIREMENTS

Vendors must demonstrate:
- Minimum 5 years experience in higher education marketing
- Portfolio of successful campaigns with measurable results
- Understanding of enrollment marketing best practices
- Capability to handle multi-channel campaigns

SECTION 4: EVALUATION CRITERIA

Proposals will be evaluated based on:
- Relevant experience (30%)
- Proposed approach (25%)
- Team qualifications (20%)
- Pricing (15%)
- References (10%)

SECTION 5: SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS

Please submit the following:
1. Company overview and qualifications
2. Detailed approach to scope of work
3. Case studies (minimum 3)
4. Pricing proposal
5. Team bios
6. Three professional references

Questions about this RFP should be directed to: procurement@abc-university.edu
